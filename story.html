Wanderlust

Khushboo, Nishant, Kay / 21 Jan 2020 / WDDM - (113-114)

It's not about merely existing. To feel alive, you have to take on the courage, move around and explore. The world is too big; forget detractors who call it a small world. Also remember that life span is very limited. Thus, how does it make sense to narrow your entire existence within a radius of few miles?

The traveling, the exploring, the experiencing of new things is what drives your soul into a higher spiritual level and life style.

Adventure is the saviour that you seek! You need not die before you find it. Close your eyes, perceive the air. Yes, smell it. That's nature calling!

No, its not space or time travel, so forget about NASA. Or wait! A quick note about NASA. Did you know NASA travelers feel bad whenever they're returning to earth from space? Famous Ed White had this to say, and of course his pains are almost tangibleI’m coming back in… and it’s the saddest moment of my life.

Must our case be like his? Not exactly. As a matter of fact, it should be the opposite. You see, it's one thing to explore space and quite another to explore the ends of the earth. Should you do one without the other? It's up for debate but the point is that man never explored earth well enough and should not be your role model, at least in the interim.

Dust your passport

Of course, how fun would it have been if we could suddenly wake up, choose our destination of resort, walk to our time travel machine and type in some magic code likerobot.magic().push() -- and poof! We've teleported. We're gone!

Not so fast bro!

"You'll never know until you go. Leave nothing But footprints. Take nothing but pictures. Kill nothing but time. But but but, no magic please"

-- Your passport

See into the far reaches

Jobs fill your pocket; adventure fill your soul. Have you had an adventure that touches your heart so deeply every time you think about it? I really hope you do because every time you remember is filling your soul on a way that can change a bad day. This topic is really one of my favorite ones because of the truth is behind it

Was this world just created to just be born, go to college, retire and die? Excuse me, but I think not so. There has to be more to human experience. A significant fraction of the world's population hate what they do for a living or how they go about their living -- and guess what? They don't tell you!
